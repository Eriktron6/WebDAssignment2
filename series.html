Star Trek: Picard
Season3

The third and final season of "Star Trek: Picard" follows the character Jean-Luc Picard in the year 2401 as he reunites with the former command crew of the USS Enterprise, including Geordi La Forge, Worf, William Riker, Beverly Crusher, Deanna Troi, and Data. They face a mysterious enemy who is hunting Picard's son. A third season of "Picard" was informally approved in January 2020, allowing it to be filmed alongside the second season. Filming for the third season took place in California and ended in March 2022. The return of other Next Generation cast members was confirmed shortly after. The season aims to provide a satisfying conclusion to Picard's story and the Next Generation cast. The season premiered on Paramount+ on February 16, 2023, and consisted of 10 episodes, running until April 20. It received positive reviews from critics and garnered nominations for Primetime Emmy Awards and other honors.

Star Trek: Strange New Worlds
Season2

The second season of "Star Trek: Strange New Worlds" follows Captain Christopher Pike and the 23rd-century Enterprise crew as they explore new worlds before "Star Trek: The Original Series." Produced by CBS Studios, showrunners Akiva Goldsman and Henry Alonso Myers maintain an episodic storytelling approach. Season 2 stars Anson Mount as Pike, Ethan Peck as Spock, and Rebecca Romijn as Number One. Confirmed in January 2022, filming occurred in Canada, featuring a crossover with "Star Trek: Lower Decks" and the franchise's first musical episode. Season 2 premiered on Paramount+ on June 15, 2023, with 10 episodes until August 10, receiving acclaim and a third season order in March 2023.

Star Trek: Lower Decks
Season 4

The fourth season of "Star Trek: Lower Decks" unfolds in the 24th century, offering a humorous take on the adventures of low-ranking officers aboard the starship Cerritos, one of Starfleet's less prestigious vessels. It is produced by CBS Eye Animation Productions in collaboration with several other production companies, led by showrunner Mike McMahan and supervising director Barry J. Kelly. Season 4 premiered on September 7, 2023, on Paramount+, releasing the first two episodes initially, followed by a weekly release of the remaining 10 episodes. In a promising sign, a fifth season was confirmed in March 2023, indicating the show's continued success and popularity among fans.

Star Trek: Prodigy
Season 2

"Star Trek: Prodigy" is an American animated TV series created by Kevin and Dan Hageman for Paramount+ and Nickelodeon. It's the tenth Star Trek series, aimed at younger audiences, and the franchise's first 3D animated series. The show follows young aliens in the 24th century who discover the abandoned starship Protostar. The series features voice talents like Brett Gray, Ella Purnell, and Kate Mulgrew reprising her role as Kathryn Janeway. Production started in 2019, with the Hageman brothers as creators and showrunners. Nickelodeon ordered two seasons in April 2019. "Prodigy" premiered on Paramount+ in October 2021 and began airing on Nickelodeon in December 2021. The first season received positive reviews and a Children's and Family Emmy Award. The second season was originally set to premiere on Paramount+ in late 2023 but was canceled in June. Netflix picked up the distribution rights, with the first season releasing in late 2023 and the second season in 2024.

Star Trek: Discovery
Season 5

The fifth and final season of "Star Trek: Discovery" continues the adventures of the starship Discovery in the 32nd century, far beyond the time of "Star Trek: The Original Series." The crew embarks on a galactic quest to uncover a long-hidden and sought-after power. Produced by CBS Studios, with showrunners Alex Kurtzman and Michelle Paradise, the season features Sonequa Martin-Green as Captain Michael Burnham and a returning ensemble cast. Filming took place in Toronto, Canada, and the season is set to premiere on Paramount+ in early 2024, with 10 episodes concluding the series.
