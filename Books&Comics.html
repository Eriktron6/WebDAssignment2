Star Trek:
Strange New Worlds: The High Country

Author: John Jackson Miller

"Star Trek: Strange New Worlds - The High Country" by John Jackson Miller, set to be released on February 21, 2023, presents a gripping narrative. Captain Christopher Pike and his crew find themselves in an unprecedented situation when Starfleet's foundational technology inexplicably ceases to function. This triggers a complex evacuation, scattering crew members across an unfamiliar world. The storyline explores the experiences of key characters, including First Officer Una and Cadet Nyota Uhura, as they navigate this novel environment. Captain Pike, while fulfilling a personal fantasy as a cowboy, remains a dedicated starship captain with significant responsibilities.This engaging narrative delves into themes of adaptation, survival, and cultural coexistence. "The High Country" promises to be a notable addition to the "Star Trek" literary universe upon its release in 2023.


Star Trek: Defiant

Creators: Cantwell/Unzueta/Cowles

In "Star Trek: Defiant," an upcoming comic set to be released in 2023, the narrative takes a unique and intriguing turn as someone begins targeting the gods themselves. As Captain Benjamin Sisko and the crew of the USS Theseus confront threats in the celestial realms, they become aware of a very real and escalating casualty count on lower ground. The true adversary in this saga is not a deity but a mortal man, a dangerous messiah leading a genocidal cult. To counter this threat, Worf of House Martok assembles his own crew aboard the USS Defiant. This formidable team, drawn from various eras of Star Trek canon, includes iconic characters such as Ro Laren, Spock, B'Elanna Torres, Lore, and more. "Star Trek: Defiant" weaves an unexpected and captivating narrative, uniting some of the most prominent personalities in the Star Trek franchise. Their mission to confront the genocidal cult leader is fraught with uncertainty and peril, promising an exciting addition to the Star Trek universe in 2023..
